Extensible Web Community Group

Join us! We're just starting…

The Extensible Web Community Group is an open source community attempting to advocate for and standardize ways that developers can extend the browser's capabilities and provide a new model for the open evolution of standards (from prollyfills to polyfills) .

Join the community group

Wondering just what a prollyfill is or what we are trying to do here? Read more about the motivations behind our project:

  • Extend the Web Forward by Yehuda Katz.
  • W3C Extensible Web Community Group: Evolving Standards by Brian Kardell.
  • How the Web disrupted Innovation by François Remy.
